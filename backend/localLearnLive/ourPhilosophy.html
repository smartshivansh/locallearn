Our Philosophy

Along with solving the problem of skilled workforce in our country with AI, Locallearn aims to be the world's first learning platform that provides a necessary and sufficient environment and support system for learners by finding the co-learners, relevant learning resources and non-judgemental digital space to share their learning obstacles. A place where

  • People connect with you with the intention of learning something from you.
  • You share your knowledge and learning journey with fellow co-learners and inspire them to learn and upgrade
  • You find people who will help you to be a better version of yourself
  • You can do what you love doing, share your thoughts freely without being judged.
  • You can learn whatever you want to and from whomever you want to.
  • People recognizes you from your skills and talent
  • You find people who can offer you jobs, internships, community projects, freelancing work, students to learn from you and you can earn money.
  • You find guidance and mentorship to help you excel in your career.
  • AI will help you find smart learning content in the form of courses, modules, videos etc. based on your interests and progress in acquiring knowledge related to certain skills.

All this and much more in the upcoming versions of Locallearn. It is a long journey and moreover a mission to unite people under one platform with their skills and knowledge. In future, Local learn is going to be a hub of skilled workforce, where you will find people with various skills and talent, collaborating with each other to find co-learners, students, mentors or guides, learning content and courses, jobs, internships, freelancers etc. everything under one roof.
